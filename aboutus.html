The story of Dog Meets Dog

Meena, Moneeza, and Ray

"The Amazing Trio"

In the Summer of 2014, Moneeza packed up her things to start her next adventure at the Kellogg School of Management. Little did she know that in a year’s time, her beloved boyfriend would end up in the frozen tundra as well! Ray applied to MBA programs and decided to attend the Kellogg School of Management! Upon moving to Heavanston (also known as Evanston) Ray completed his family by bringing his beloved Yorkie Princess named Meena!

Meena, Moneeza, and Ray (Dubbed “The Amazing Trio”) are delighted to bring you “Dog Meets Dog”! A site that is dedicated to helping you meet other pet owners to set up play dates for your furry friends and who knows… possibly find another pet owner to become the love of your life! Owners and Pets both look and act alike, and if you are like us, our pets are family and they too should have some fun in life! ☺

We hope you enjoy Dog Meets Dog. We would love to see what else we could do to make Dog Meets Dog work better for you.
